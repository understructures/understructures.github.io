oberih

is a publishing and fundraising project that serves as a platform of support for people affected by the war in Ukraine. it is a collection of stories about oberihs [ukr.: ‘an item that protects’],— things that accompany us and provide solace and protection in all circumstances. gathered from our friends, who are fighting, working, volunteering and directly facing the consequences of the war, these stories reached us in the form of short texts, images, voice messages and online posts in the period of april–may 2022.

Initatives we support

All proceedings from donations and sales of the edition will be entirely directed towards volunteer initiatives and humanitarian organisations, which operate in territories affected by the war and are related to some of our contributors.

  • ateliernormalno
    (a group of artists with and without Down syndrome; two artists from the group, Katya Libkind and Stas Turina, are working and volunteering in various initiatives and foundations that support people with disabilities)

  • Ukraine Helping Medical Collection
    (a joint group of Polish and Ukrainian citizens that coordinates a collection of funds, specific medical and tactical products in Poland. The collected funds are used to purchase the most necessary products, according to the demands of hospitals and auxiliary wards)

  • Hospitaliers
    (a volunteer organisation of paramedics working directly on the front line)

  • IT-Troups
    (a volunteer organisation providing a wide range of humanitarian aid and assisting media coverage of the war in Ukraine)

  • Women Veteran Movement
    (a consolidation of female veterans, who advocate protection of the rights of women amongst veterans and active military personnel, and aim to create opportunities for their self-realisation in civil life)
